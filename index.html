BEFORE BOOKING

We want to make this as simple as possible. Please read these brief guidelines before filling out the form.

We strictly service public locations and official venues (event halls, hotels, restaurants, corporate offices, etc.). Please do not fill out the form if your event is located at a private residence.
"Strips" are standard 4x6 prints that contain two identical strips of the exact same picture. So if you take 1 photo, the machine prints 1 sheet containing 2 strips so you can split it with a friend. Our $90/hr rate gives you up to 50 prints maximum. If you want unlimited prints, it's a flat $50 upgrade.
There is NO delivery fee for our core service area! We exclusively serve: Port Richey down to St. Pete (the Pasco/Pinellas side), Wesley Chapel, all of Tampa and Hillsborough County, and within 45 minutes of Tampa (such as Brandon or South Tampa).
When you click the booking form, please have the following ready:

1. The exact Date and Time of the event.
2. The exact number of hours you want to rent it for.
3. The name and address of the PUBLIC VENUE.
4. Whether you want the FREE props and inflatable backdrop included!
